# Sarasota Independent Restaurant Route
## For Shift4 Solution Specialist Field Assignment — 2026-04-17

**Goal:** Visit 3+ locally-owned, independent, full-service restaurants. No chains.
**Minimum:** 3 visits. **Recommended:** 5-6 visits so you can pick the strongest 3 for the pitch.

---

## Route strategy

All candidates are grouped into **4 legs**, ordered so you never cross town twice. Total drive time end-to-end ≈ 35-40 min (without stops). Recommended sequence: **Leg A → B → C → D**, or just pick the 2-3 closest legs to your start point.

> **⚠️ Verify before you drive:** These are well-known Sarasota independents as of this writing, but restaurants close, change hands, or switch concepts. Call ahead or check Google Maps to confirm hours and "still open" status before each stop. Full-service means sit-down with servers — skip anything that's gone counter-service or fast-casual.

## LEG A — Downtown Sarasota (Main St / Palm Ave / 1st St)
*Walkable cluster — park once, hit 3 in 45 min.*

### 1. Bijou Café
- **Address:** 1287 1st St, Sarasota, FL 34236
- **Type:** French / American, fine dining
- **Est.:** 1986 (40 years — gold for the "how long in business" answer)
- **Why visit:** One of the oldest independents in Sarasota. Owner Jean-Pierre Knaggs is a known operator. Likely Aloha or Toast.

### 2. Selva Grill
- **Address:** 1345 Main St, Sarasota, FL 34236
- **Type:** Peruvian / Latin fusion
- **Why visit:** Owner-operated, single location, active bar program (high POS volume).

### 3. Owen's Fish Camp
- **Address:** 516 Burns Ct, Sarasota, FL 34236
- **Type:** Southern seafood
- **Why visit:** Iconic local independent, always packed. Indie group (Tableseide) — worth asking about number of locations; if they own 2-3 under one roof, still qualifies as locally-owned.

### 4. Duval's Fresh. Local. Seafood.
- **Address:** 1435 Main St, Sarasota, FL 34236
- **Type:** Seafood, farm-to-table
- **Why visit:** Duval family operation, single location, strong reputation.

### 5. Louies Modern
- **Address:** 1289 N Palm Ave, Sarasota, FL 34236
- **Type:** American modern / bistro
- **Why visit:** Part of a small 2-location local group — ask GM directly.

The "longevity" angle — all 3 restaurants with 35+ years in business, which makes the "why haven't you upgraded your POS?" question land hard:

1. **Cafe L'Europe** (est. 1973, St. Armands) — 53 years

Drive order: Downtown (Bijou) → Southside (Michael's) → St. Armands (L'Europe). ~25 min total drive.
